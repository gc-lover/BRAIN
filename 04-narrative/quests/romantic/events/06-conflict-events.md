### Conflict Severity
- **Minor (1-3)** ‚Äî Small argument, easy to resolve
- **Moderate (4-6)** ‚Äî Serious fight, needs reconciliation event
- **Major (7-9)** ‚Äî Relationship-threatening, may need multiple reconciliation
- **Critical (10)** ‚Äî Break-up crisis, may be unrecoverable

### Conflict Resolution Paths
1. **Immediate apology** ‚Äî Quick fix (DC lower)
2. **Give space** ‚Äî Cool off period, then reconcile
3. **Grand gesture** ‚Äî Big apology (DC higher but +relationship if success)
4. **Therapy/Mediation** ‚Äî Third party help (Empathy DC 22)
5. **Unresolved** ‚Äî Leads to more conflicts, eventual crisis

### Conflict accumulation
```
Unresolved_Conflicts = Count of conflicts not resolved
If Unresolved_Conflicts >= 3: Trigger RE-201 (Crisis/Breakup)
If Unresolved_Conflicts >= 5: Relationship ends (unless major reconciliation)
```

### Make-up events
- After conflict resolved ‚Üí RE-166-185 (Reconciliation events)
- "Make-up sex" ‚Äî +40 relationship, Passion after fight
- "I'm sorry" kiss ‚Äî +25 relationship, Sweet reconciliation
- Long talk resolution ‚Äî +30 relationship, Understanding reached
